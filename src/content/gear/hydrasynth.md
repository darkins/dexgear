## Polytouch® Keybed

The Hydrasynth Explorer has a 37-note keybed with **full polyphonic aftertouch per key** — every key independently detects continuous pressure after the initial strike. This is in addition to mono aftertouch and standard Note-On/Off velocity. Battery powered (8× AA batteries) for portable, standalone use away from the studio.

## Sound Engine — Oscillators

<ul class="space-y-4 list-disc pl-6">
  <li><strong>3 oscillators</strong> with a library of 219 single-cycle waveforms.</li>
  <li>Oscillators 1 and 2 feature <strong>WAVELIST wavemorphing</strong> — select any 8 of the 219 waves to build a custom morph chain; any mod source (LFO, envelope, poly AT, Slide) can scan through the list in real time.</li>
  <li>Oscillator bit depth is selectable in v2.0 — reduce to 8-bit or 12-bit for vintage lo-fi character without additional effects.</li>
  <li><strong>Wave Mutants</strong> on Osc 1 and 2: FM-Linear, WavStack™ (5 detuned copies), Osc Sync, PW-Orig, PW-Squeeze, PW-ASM (three pulse width flavours), Harmonic Sweep, and PhazDiff (phase inversion and shifting).</li>
</ul>

## Mixer

Five sound sources (Osc 1, Osc 2, Osc 3, Noise, and Ring Mod) feed into the Mixer section with independent level and stereo pan controls. Each source can be routed independently to Filter 1, Filter 2, or bypassed entirely — enabling complex parallel filter architectures.

## Filters

<ul class="space-y-4 list-disc pl-6">
  <li><strong>Filter 1:</strong> 16 selectable filter models covering 1–8 pole, High-Pass, Band-Pass, Low-Pass, and two vocal/formant types.</li>
  <li><strong>Filter 2:</strong> 12dB/oct SEM-style state-variable filter — an independent character to contrast or complement Filter 1.</li>
  <li>Filters run in <strong>series or parallel</strong>; use parallel routing to send the left output through Filter 1 only and the right through Filter 2 for fundamentally different stereo characters per output.</li>
</ul>

## Modulation

<ul class="space-y-4 list-disc pl-6">
  <li><strong>5 LFOs:</strong> STEP mode (up to 64 programmable steps — functions as a mini sequencer), 10 standard waveforms, BPM sync, One-Shot mode (acts as a triggered envelope), and per-step quantize (v2.0). Route any LFO to wavetable position for animated timbral movement.</li>
  <li><strong>5 DAHDSR Envelopes:</strong> 6 stages (Delay, Attack, Hold, Decay, Sustain, Release). ADR stages can sync to BPM subdivisions. Envelopes are loopable — give them a tempo-synced loop time to convert them into rhythmic LFOs. Quantize available in v2.0.</li>
  <li><strong>32-slot Modulation Matrix:</strong> 35 modulation sources × 191 destinations. CV Mod Out jacks and all 128 MIDI CCs are available as both sources and destinations — map incoming CC from Knobbler sliders directly to synthesis parameters without any DAW involvement.</li>
</ul>

## Arpeggiator

Standard direction modes (up, down, up/down, random) plus two unique modes: **RATCHET** (rhythmic subdivisions within a single step) and **CHANCE** (each step has a probabilistic chance of playing). Step Offset (v2.0) shifts the arpeggio starting position. The arpeggiator has a 6-octave range and most of its parameters are themselves mod matrix destinations.

## CV/Gate Connectivity

- **1V/oct CV In** and **CV Out** (Buchla 1.2V/oct and Japanese Hz/V alternatives selectable)
- **Gate In** and **Gate Out**
- **CV Mod Out** jacks extend into audio frequency range — use as audio-rate oscillator inputs for wild carrier modulation in a Eurorack system.

## Macros

Two pages of 4 encoder+button pairs each provide **8 Macro controls** per patch. Each Macro can control up to 8 destinations simultaneously from a single encoder turn. Assign the 8 Macros to targets like filter cutoff + resonance, two LFO rates, and an envelope amount for a compact live performance control surface that Knobbler can display with auto-labels.

## Patches and Browser

- 1024 presets (8 banks × 128 patches)
- 32-slot Favorites list for quick-access performance patches
- **INIT** button — instantly initialise a blank patch for sound design from scratch
- **RANDOM** button — generate a randomised patch as a starting point (hold for partial randomisation options)
- Patch Manager app (Win/Mac) for desktop backup and sharing

## Effects Chain

A pre-effects and post-effects chain includes: Pitch FX, Lo-Fi, EQ, Distortion, modeled Delays (BPM sync), and modeled Reverbs. The full chain is internal — no MOTU processing power needed for the Hydrasynth's own effects.

## Integration

<ul class="space-y-4 list-disc pl-6">
  <li><strong>Audio out:</strong> balanced stereo TRS → MOTU inputs 5/6 (or inputs 3/4 if the VT-3 is not in use). Use Live's External Instrument device to monitor the Hydrasynth with zero latency.</li>
  <li><strong>MIDI:</strong> USB MIDI to Ableton, or 5-pin DIN In/Out via MOTU's rear MIDI ports for a hardware-only signal chain.</li>
  <li><strong>Seaboard M MPE control:</strong> route Seaboard M MIDI output to the Hydrasynth's USB MIDI input. The Hydrasynth's poly aftertouch engine responds to Seaboard M's Press dimension on a per-voice basis — full MPE expressivity across 8 voices.</li>
  <li><strong>Knobbler macro control:</strong> map Knobbler sliders #1–8 to the Hydrasynth's 8 Macro CCs for labelled, real-time synthesis control from the iPad without taking hands off the keybed.</li>
  <li><strong>Launchkey 37 Scale Mode:</strong> match the Launchkey's Scale Mode root key to the Hydrasynth patch's tuning for zero wrong-note keyboard layering across both controllers simultaneously.</li>
</ul>
